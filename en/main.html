FAQ

What does the cleaning include?

Cleaning is agreed in advance and typically includes dusting, vacuuming, floor washing, kitchen and bathroom cleaning.

How often can cleaning be booked?

One-time or recurring — weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly.

Do I need to be home during cleaning?

No. Secure access can be arranged.

Do you bring equipment and supplies?

Yes. We use our own professional tools and products.

Is the service insured?

Yes. Work is performed responsibly and carefully.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

Yes. Please notify at least 24 hours in advance.

Privacy Policy – BEL Cleaning Services

BEL Cleaning Services respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data Controller

BEL Cleaning Services
Email: bel.siivous@gmail.com
Phone: +358 45 861 5711
Service Area: Kotka – Hamina – Finland

2. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following personal data:

Name

Phone number

Email address

Address or service location

Billing information

Information submitted via contact forms

Customer communication

We only collect information necessary for providing our services.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

Personal data is processed solely for the following purposes:

Responding to inquiries and requests

Providing and managing cleaning services

Preparing offers and handling billing

Maintaining customer relationships

Fulfilling legal obligations (accounting, taxation)

Personal data is never sold or shared for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on:

Contract – when services are ordered

Consent – when contacting us

Legal obligation – compliance with Finnish laws

Legitimate interest – customer service and business operations

5. Data Retention Period

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary:

Inquiries and requests: up to 12 months

Customer communication: up to 24 months

Billing and accounting data: as required by Finnish law (typically 6 years)

Unnecessary data is regularly deleted.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Personal data may be processed by trusted service providers such as:

Billing and accounting systems

Email and communication services

Website platform providers (Webflow)

Data is never disclosed for advertising or marketing purposes.

7. International Data Transfers

Personal data is generally not transferred outside the EU/EEA. If technical services require transfers, GDPR-compliant safeguards are applied.

8. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to:

Ensure website functionality

Improve user experience

Collect basic analytics data

Users may disable cookies through browser settings.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to:

Access your personal data

Request correction of inaccurate data

Request deletion of your data

Withdraw consent at any time

Object to data processing

Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

Requests may be submitted via email.

10. Contact Regarding Privacy

For any privacy-related matters:

bel.siivous@gmail.com

FAQ

What does the cleaning include?

Cleaning is agreed in advance and typically includes dusting, vacuuming, floor washing, kitchen and bathroom cleaning.

How often can cleaning be booked?

One-time or recurring — weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly.

Do I need to be home during cleaning?

No. Secure access can be arranged.

Do you bring equipment and supplies?

Yes. We use our own professional tools and products.

Is the service insured?

Yes. Work is performed responsibly and carefully.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

Yes. Please notify at least 24 hours in advance.
